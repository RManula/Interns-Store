# People & Culture Internship - NAB

## Role Description
NAB's People & Culture team supports a workforce of 37,000+ NABers across Australia and internationally, covering talent acquisition, people analytics, learning & development, inclusion & diversity, and employee experience. As a People & Culture intern, you'll contribute to programs that make NAB a workplace where people love to work and are empowered to do their best.

## Key Responsibilities
* Support talent acquisition operations including job posting, candidate screening, interview coordination, and onboarding logistics for graduate and experienced hire programs
* Assist the People Analytics team in building workforce reporting dashboards and analysing turnover, engagement, and diversity metrics
* Contribute to NAB's inclusion & diversity initiatives including awareness campaigns, ERG event coordination, and inclusive hiring audit projects
* Assist in designing and delivering content for NAB's internal learning platform (Degreed) and leadership development programs

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Understanding of HR management principles, talent acquisition, and learning & development best practices
* Excel and Power BI skills for HR data analysis and people dashboard development
* Strong interpersonal and communication skills with high emotional intelligence
* Enrolled in Human Resources, I/O Psychology, Business, Commerce, or Education (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Broad exposure across talent, analytics, diversity, and L&D within a major Australian employer
* Access to NAB's Workday HCM and Degreed learning platforms for hands-on system experience
* NAB team member benefits including subsidised health insurance, discounted NAB banking products, and EAP
* Pathway to NAB's People & Culture Graduate Program or specialist HR analyst roles
