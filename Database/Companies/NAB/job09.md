# Quantitative Analytics Internship - NAB

## Role Description
NAB's Quantitative Analytics (QA) team develops and maintains the mathematical models that power NAB's credit risk, market risk, capital adequacy, and pricing frameworks, operating within APRA's Basel III/IV regulatory environment. As a Quantitative Analytics intern, you'll work on model development, validation, or research tasks alongside PhD-qualified quants in one of Australia's most technically rigorous banking environments.

## Key Responsibilities
* Contribute to credit risk model development or validation — including PD, LGD, EAD models for retail, SME, or corporate portfolios
* Implement quantitative models in Python and perform statistical backtesting, performance monitoring, and model documentation
* Conduct literature reviews and research emerging methodologies in credit risk, XVA, or stress testing
* Support APRA regulatory submissions including Basel Pillar 3 disclosures and internal capital adequacy assessments

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Strong quantitative foundation: probability, mathematical statistics, stochastic processes, and numerical methods
* Python (NumPy, pandas, scipy, statsmodels) or R for quantitative model implementation
* Understanding of credit risk, Basel regulatory capital, or interest rate modelling is highly advantageous
* Enrolled in Applied Mathematics, Statistics, Financial Engineering, Actuarial Studies, Physics, or Econometrics (penultimate or final year; postgraduate students very welcome)

## Perks & Benefits
* Exposure to regulatory-grade quantitative modelling within Australia's largest business bank
* Access to NAB's quant model library and financial risk research database
* Pathway to NAB's Quantitative Analytics Graduate stream — starting salary $87,000+ AUD
* Collaboration with NAB's global quant network spanning Melbourne, Sydney, and London
