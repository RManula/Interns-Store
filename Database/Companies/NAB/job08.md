# Business Banking Internship - NAB

## Role Description
NAB is Australia's largest business bank, supporting over 700,000 small-to-medium businesses, agribusinesses, and mid-market companies with lending, transaction banking, trade finance, and advisory services. As a Business Banking intern in Sydney, you'll embed with a regional business banking team working directly with Australian entrepreneurs, operators, and business owners.

## Key Responsibilities
* Shadow Business Banking Managers in client meetings, lending assessments, and annual business reviews
* Prepare credit analysis, business health assessments, and financial statement summaries for SME loan applications
* Support the team in preparing proposals for business banking products including overdrafts, equipment finance, and commercial property lending
* Contribute to internal portfolio reviews tracking credit quality, covenant compliance, and growth opportunities across the team's client book

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Foundational knowledge of business accounting, financial statement analysis, and SME credit principles
* Excel proficiency for financial modelling and business performance analysis
* Strong communication and relationship-building skills for client-facing interactions
* Enrolled in Commerce, Finance, Business, Accounting, or Economics (penultimate year preferred)

## Perks & Benefits
* Direct client exposure from week two — rare and accelerated for a banking internship
* Mentorship from senior Business Banking Managers and regional banking leadership
* Access to NAB's Business Banking training academy and credit assessment certification program
* Clear pathway to NAB's Business Banking Analyst Graduate stream ($72,000+ AUD package)
