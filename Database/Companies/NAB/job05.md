# FinTech & Digital Innovation Internship - NAB

## Role Description
NAB Ventures and NAB's Digital & Innovation team invest in and incubate fintech solutions that reimagine banking for Australian consumers and businesses. As a FinTech & Digital Innovation intern, you'll work at the intersection of banking, technology, and entrepreneurship — exploring open banking, embedded finance, AI, and payments innovation within a Big Four bank's innovation engine.

## Key Responsibilities
* Research and evaluate emerging fintech trends and startup ecosystems relevant to NAB's strategic priorities in payments, lending, and business banking
* Build proof-of-concept prototypes integrating NAB's open banking APIs (CDR-compliant) and third-party fintech partner capabilities
* Support NAB Ventures' deal pipeline analysis including competitive intelligence, market sizing, and investment thesis development
* Participate in innovation sprints, design thinking workshops, and customer co-creation sessions

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* API integration experience with REST concepts; Postman or similar tools
* Basic coding ability in Python or JavaScript for rapid prototyping
* Understanding of open banking (Consumer Data Right), payments regulation, and Australian fintech landscape
* Enrolled in FinTech, Computer Science, Finance, Business, or Information Systems (any year considered)

## Perks & Benefits
* Access to NAB Ventures' portfolio of Australian and international fintech investments for research and networking
* Work from NAB's Innovation Hub at 700 Bourke Street, Melbourne — a dedicated startup-style environment within the bank
* $400 AUD innovation budget for courses, tools, or fintech conference attendance
* Fast-track consideration for NAB's Technology or Digital Graduate Program
