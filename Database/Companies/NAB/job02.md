# Cybersecurity Internship - NAB

## Role Description
NAB's Cyber Defence Centre is one of the most sophisticated bank cybersecurity operations in Australia, protecting the financial data of over 9 million customers and critical banking infrastructure from increasingly complex cyber threats. As a Cybersecurity intern, you'll join a specialised team working across threat detection, vulnerability management, or identity security within NAB's enterprise security ecosystem.

## Key Responsibilities
* Monitor NAB's Security Operations Centre (SOC) environment and assist with alert triage in Splunk SIEM
* Conduct threat intelligence research tracking adversary groups targeting the Australian financial services sector
* Support vulnerability management processes including scanning, prioritisation, and remediation tracking across NAB's IT estate
* Contribute to security awareness campaign development and phishing simulation program coordination

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Foundational cybersecurity knowledge covering network security, malware analysis, and MITRE ATT&CK framework
* Familiarity with SIEM platforms (Splunk preferred) and security monitoring workflows
* Python or PowerShell scripting for security automation and log analysis
* Enrolled in Cybersecurity, Computer Science, Information Technology, or related degree (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Exposure to enterprise security operations at one of Australia's Big Four banks
* Sponsored GIAC, CompTIA, or Microsoft Security certification preparation and exam voucher
* Hybrid work with access to NAB's state-of-the-art Docklands headquarters in Melbourne
* Strong internship-to-graduate conversion pathway into NAB's Cyber Defence Graduate Program
