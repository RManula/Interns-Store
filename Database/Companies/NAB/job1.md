# Corporate Banking Internship - NAB

## Role Description
NAB's Corporate & Institutional Banking (CIB) division is one of Australia's leading corporate finance and advisory franchises, providing debt markets, transaction banking, and strategic advisory services to Australia's largest corporations, government entities, and infrastructure owners. As a Corporate Banking intern, you'll join a sector coverage team gaining real deal exposure from day one.

## Key Responsibilities
* Assist analysts in building financial models for leveraged buyout, infrastructure financing, and real estate debt transactions
* Prepare credit submissions, investment committee papers, and information memorandums for new and existing credit facilities
* Conduct industry and company research to support origination pitches and client relationship reviews
* Support transaction execution activities including due diligence coordination, legal documentation review, and settlement logistics

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Strong financial modelling skills in Excel including DCF, leveraged finance, and project finance models
* Understanding of corporate credit analysis, banking products, and Australian capital markets
* Excellent written communication for producing client-ready documents and internal submissions
* Enrolled in Finance, Commerce, Economics, Accounting, or Law (penultimate year strongly preferred)

## Perks & Benefits
* Exposure to marquee Australian M&A, infrastructure, and real estate debt transactions
* Mentorship from Executive Directors and MDs with NAB's sector coverage teams
* Access to NAB's Bloomberg terminal, Refinitiv, and internal deal database
* Priority consideration for NAB's Corporate & Institutional Banking Graduate Analyst Program
