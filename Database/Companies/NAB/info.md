### 3.2. AI Summary of Recent Reviews
Employees appreciate their colleagues, who are generally friendly and supportive, as well as the excellent modern office facilities and central locations in Sydney and Melbourne. Flexible work-from-home (WFH) arrangements are widely available and help with overall work-life balance. Competitive employee benefits, including base salaries and corporate bonuses, are highly valued.

**The Challenges:** Employees have noted potential challenges around management support, high workloads, and strict operational KPIs with a heavy sales focus. Understaffing in specific frontline units can occasionally lead to unpaid overtime. Additionally, frequent organizational restructures can affect local team structures and overall job security.
