# Risk & Compliance Internship - NAB

## Role Description
NAB's Enterprise Risk Management function oversees the frameworks that govern how NAB manages credit, market, operational, and non-financial risks across its Australian and international operations. As a Risk & Compliance intern, you'll gain hands-on exposure to banking risk governance within one of Australia's most rigorous risk management organisations, operating under APRA, ASIC, and AUSTRAC oversight.

## Key Responsibilities
* Support risk teams in preparing risk committee papers, RCSA updates, and operational risk event analyses
* Research and document regulatory change impacts from APRA prudential standards updates, ASIC guidance, and AML/CTF legislation amendments
* Assist in model risk governance activities including model inventory maintenance and validation support documentation
* Contribute to NAB's compliance assurance testing program, including control testing workpapers and findings reports

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Understanding of banking risk frameworks, APRA prudential standards, and compliance management principles
* Analytical skills with Excel proficiency for risk data analysis and scenario modelling
* Excellent written communication for preparing board-level risk documentation and regulatory submissions
* Enrolled in Finance, Economics, Law, Commerce, Risk Management, or Actuarial Studies (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Structured rotation across credit risk, operational risk, or compliance teams based on business need
* Access to NAB's Risk Academy internal training curriculum and APRA regulatory engagement updates
* Study support for FRM Part I (GARP), CAMS (AML), or CRISC certifications post-internship
* Pathway to NAB's Risk Management Graduate Program — one of the most respected risk grad programs in Australian banking
