# Financial Analytics Internship - NAB

## Role Description
NAB's Finance Analytics & Reporting team produces the financial intelligence that powers NAB Group's decision-making across retail banking, business banking, institutional markets, and wealth. As a Financial Analytics intern, you'll work alongside senior finance analysts building models, automating reports, and generating insights from NAB's enterprise financial data.

## Key Responsibilities
* Develop and maintain financial reporting models in Excel and Python tracking NAB Group KPIs across net interest margin, cost-to-income ratio, and return on equity
* Automate recurring finance reporting workflows using Python and SQL to reduce manual effort and improve data accuracy
* Build Power BI dashboards for divisional CFOs and business unit Finance Business Partners
* Conduct ad hoc financial analysis supporting NAB's half-year and full-year results preparation process

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Strong SQL skills for extracting and transforming financial data from NAB's enterprise data warehouse
* Python (pandas, openpyxl) proficiency for financial automation and data processing
* Power BI or Tableau skills for finance dashboard development
* Enrolled in Finance, Accounting, Data Science, Actuarial Studies, or Economics (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Exposure to financial reporting and analytics at an ASX20 bank with $70B+ in annual revenue
* Access to NAB's enterprise financial systems and data analytics training curriculum
* Mentorship from NAB's senior finance team and CFO organisation leadership
* Strong pathway to NAB's Finance & Analytics Graduate Program or Quantitative Finance stream
