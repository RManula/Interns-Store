# Supply Chain Internship - BHP

## Role Description
BHP's Supply Chain function manages one of the world's most complex industrial procurement and logistics operations — sourcing billions of dollars worth of equipment, consumables, and services to keep BHP's global mining operations running. As a Supply Chain intern, you'll work within BHP's Perth-based supply chain team supporting procurement, materials management, or logistics operations for Western Australian assets.

## Key Responsibilities
* Assist procurement teams in supplier evaluation, tendering, and contract analysis for goods and services across BHP's WA operations
* Analyse materials inventory data to identify slow-moving stock, critical spares gaps, and replenishment optimisation opportunities
* Support logistics coordination for major equipment movements between BHP's mine sites, ports, and maintenance facilities
* Contribute to supply chain continuous improvement projects using lean methodologies to reduce cost and improve service levels

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Understanding of procurement, strategic sourcing, and supply chain management principles
* Excel proficiency for inventory analysis, demand forecasting, and spend analytics
* Strong analytical and problem-solving skills for complex multi-variable supply chain challenges
* Enrolled in Supply Chain Management, Industrial Engineering, Commerce, Mining Engineering, or Logistics (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Exposure to global-scale industrial supply chain management at one of the world's largest mining companies
* Site visit program to BHP's WA iron ore and nickel operations — see the supply chain end-to-end
* FIFO site trip allowances and Perth CBD accommodation subsidy for interstate interns
* Pathway to BHP's Supply Chain Graduate Program and global procurement specialist career track
