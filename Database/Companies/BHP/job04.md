# Digital R&D Internship - BHP

## Role Description
BHP's Digital R&D team researches and prototypes advanced digital solutions that push the boundaries of what's possible in mining and resources — from AI-driven predictive maintenance and digital twins to computer vision for ore quality assessment and generative AI for engineering knowledge management. As a Digital R&D intern, you'll work on exploratory projects at the frontier of industrial AI and digital innovation.

## Key Responsibilities
* Research and prototype AI/ML solutions for specific operational problems such as predictive equipment maintenance, process optimisation, or geological modelling
* Build and evaluate machine learning models using Python (PyTorch, scikit-learn, Hugging Face) and validate against operational datasets from BHP mine sites
* Contribute to digital twin or simulation models of mining processes using physics-based and data-driven hybrid modelling approaches
* Present research outcomes, prototype demonstrations, and technical findings to BHP's Digital R&D leadership at the intern showcase

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Strong Python programming skills with experience in machine learning frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow, or scikit-learn)
* Understanding of ML model development lifecycle including data preparation, training, evaluation, and deployment
* Curiosity and research aptitude — ability to read academic papers and translate findings into practical prototypes
* Enrolled in Computer Science, AI/ML, Data Science, Electrical Engineering, or Applied Mathematics (penultimate or final year; postgraduate students strongly encouraged)

## Perks & Benefits
* Work on genuinely novel AI problems with direct industrial application and potential for patents or publications
* Access to BHP's GPU compute resources and Azure ML environment for experimentation
* Mentorship from BHP's Senior Research Scientists, Principal Engineers, and Distinguished Engineers
* Potential to continue research collaboration post-internship through BHP's university research partnership programs
