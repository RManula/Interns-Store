# Health, Safety & Environment (HSE) Internship - BHP

## Role Description
At BHP, the safety, health, and wellbeing of our people and the communities we operate in is our highest priority. BHP's HSE function manages risk, develops safety systems, and drives a culture of care and accountability across operations in some of the world's most challenging environments. As an HSE intern, you'll contribute to programs that protect the lives of thousands of BHP workers across Australian mining operations.

## Key Responsibilities
* Support HSE teams in conducting risk assessments, hazard identification reviews, and bow-tie analyses for operational activities at Australian mine sites
* Assist in the analysis of safety incident data, near-miss trends, and leading indicator metrics to identify patterns and drive preventive action
* Contribute to the development and review of critical control procedures, safety management plans, and HSE training materials
* Participate in site HSE inspections, safety conversations, and field verification activities during site exposure visits

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Understanding of occupational health and safety management systems (ISO 45001, OHSAS 18001) and risk assessment methodologies
* Data analysis skills in Excel for safety performance reporting and trend analysis
* Strong written communication for producing clear, accessible HSE documentation and incident reports
* Enrolled in Occupational Health & Safety, Environmental Science, Mining Engineering, Chemical Engineering, or related degree (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Site exposure visits to BHP's operating mine sites in Western Australia — rare and invaluable for HSE careers
* Access to BHP's global HSE knowledge base, fatal risk control protocols, and world-class safety management systems
* FIFO site trip support and Perth-based housing allowance for interstate candidates
* Pathway to BHP's HSE Graduate Program and safety leadership career track across BHP's global operations
