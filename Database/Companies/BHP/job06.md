# Geoscience & Data Science Internship - BHP

## Role Description
BHP's Geoscience team in Adelaide applies cutting-edge data science and computational geology to support exploration, resource estimation, and mine planning across BHP's global copper, nickel, and iron ore assets. As a Geoscience & Data Science intern, you'll work at the intersection of earth sciences and advanced analytics to help BHP find and characterise the ore deposits of the future.

## Key Responsibilities
* Apply machine learning and geostatistical methods to geological datasets for mineral prospectivity mapping and resource characterisation
* Develop and validate predictive models for geological properties (grade, lithology, geotechnical characteristics) using drill hole and geophysical data
* Build data visualisation tools and dashboards for geological data exploration using Python and BHP's internal geoscience platforms
* Support research into novel data integration methods combining remote sensing, geochemistry, and geophysical data streams

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Quantitative background with strong Python or R skills for geospatial data analysis and machine learning
* Familiarity with geoscience software (Leapfrog, ArcGIS, QGIS) or willingness to learn rapidly
* Understanding of geological concepts, mineral systems, or resource estimation is highly advantageous
* Enrolled in Geological Science, Geophysics, Data Science, or Applied Mathematics (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Access to BHP's global exploration datasets and geoscience knowledge base
* Mentorship from BHP's senior geoscientists, chief geologists, and data science specialists
* Adelaide-based with potential exposure visits to BHP's South Australian copper operations (Olympic Dam, Carrapateena)
* Pathway to BHP's Geoscience Graduate Program or Data Science Specialist track within the resources sector
