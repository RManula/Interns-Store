# Finance Internship - BHP

## Role Description
BHP Group's Finance function manages the financial operations of one of the world's largest diversified resources companies, with revenues exceeding $50 billion USD annually. As a Finance intern based in BHP's Melbourne headquarters, you'll gain exposure to financial planning & analysis, treasury, investor relations, or corporate finance within a truly global ASX and LSE dual-listed company.

## Key Responsibilities
* Support the FP&A team with monthly and quarterly performance reporting, business unit P&L analysis, and commodity-level financial modelling
* Assist in preparing materials for BHP's bi-annual results announcements, investor day presentations, and Board Financial Committee papers
* Contribute to treasury operations including cash positioning, debt portfolio reporting, and foreign exchange risk analysis
* Build and maintain financial models for capital allocation decisions, major project approvals, and commodity price sensitivity analyses

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Advanced Excel skills for financial modelling including commodity price scenarios, net asset value, and operating cost analysis
* Understanding of mining company financial structures, including production costs (C1/AISC), royalties, and capital intensity
* Foundational understanding of IFRS accounting standards and corporate finance principles
* Enrolled in Finance, Accounting, Commerce, or Economics (penultimate year strongly preferred; CA/CPA pathway)

## Perks & Benefits
* Finance experience at one of the world's top five most valuable mining companies listed on both ASX and LSE
* Exposure to BHP's global FP&A and investor relations processes including analyst day preparation
* Study support for CA or CPA program and access to BHP's extensive finance professional development curriculum
* BHP Melbourne headquarters facilities, competitive stipend, and full superannuation contributions
