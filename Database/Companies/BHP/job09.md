# Environment & Sustainability Internship - BHP

## Role Description
BHP's Environment & Sustainability team leads the company's commitments to climate action, biodiversity protection, water stewardship, and closure planning across its global mining operations. As an Environment & Sustainability intern in Perth, you'll contribute to programs that balance BHP's resource development activities with environmental responsibility and community commitments in Western Australia.

## Key Responsibilities
* Assist in compiling Scope 1, 2, and 3 greenhouse gas emissions data and supporting BHP's annual sustainability reporting (TCFD, GRI, SASB aligned)
* Support environmental impact assessment processes for new projects or operational changes requiring regulatory approval
* Contribute to biodiversity offset and rehabilitation monitoring programs at BHP's WA operations
* Research and document best practices in water management, tailings storage facility governance, and land access management

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Understanding of environmental management systems, impact assessment methodologies, and Australian environmental legislation (EPBC Act, State EP Acts)
* Data analysis skills in Excel or Python for environmental monitoring data processing and reporting
* Strong written communication for preparing technical reports, regulatory submissions, and stakeholder communications
* Enrolled in Environmental Science, Environmental Engineering, Sustainability, Ecology, or Natural Resource Management (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Exposure to environmental management at one of the world's most scrutinised and progressive mining companies
* Site visits to BHP's WA operations to observe environmental programs and rehabilitation activities firsthand
* FIFO site trip support, Perth accommodation allowance, and mentorship from BHP's Principal Environmental Scientists
* Pathway to BHP's Environment Graduate Program and specialist environmental and closure career tracks
