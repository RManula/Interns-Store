# Mining Technology Internship - BHP

## Role Description
BHP's Mining Technology team is at the forefront of applying automation, robotics, and digital technology to make mining safer, more productive, and more sustainable at some of the world's largest mine sites. As a Mining Technology intern, you'll be based in Perth and work with teams deploying autonomous haulage systems, remote operations technology, and digital mine solutions across BHP's Pilbara iron ore operations.

## Key Responsibilities
* Support the deployment and performance monitoring of autonomous haulage systems (AHS) including data analysis and fault resolution support
* Assist in developing digital dashboards and operational intelligence tools that give mine controllers real-time visibility of production performance
* Contribute to technology integration projects connecting mine control systems, fleet management tools, and remote operations centres
* Document technical specifications, system configurations, and operational runbooks for mining technology systems

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Engineering or computer science fundamentals with exposure to industrial control systems, IoT, or automation technology
* Programming proficiency in Python for data analysis, scripting, and system monitoring tasks
* Understanding of mining operations, ore extraction processes, or heavy industrial environments is advantageous
* Enrolled in Mining Engineering, Mechatronics, Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, or related degree (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Site visits to BHP's world-class Pilbara iron ore operations — a once-in-a-career experience
* Exposure to the world's most advanced autonomous mining technology fleet
* BHP internship allowances including fly-in fly-out (FIFO) site trip support and Perth CBD accommodation subsidy
* Pathway to BHP's Mining Technology Graduate program and global technical career track
