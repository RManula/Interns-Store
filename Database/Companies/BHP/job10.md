# Automation & Control Systems Internship - BHP

## Role Description
BHP's Automation & Control Systems team designs and maintains the industrial control systems, SCADA networks, and process automation solutions that keep BHP's processing plants, ports, and rail operations running safely and efficiently. As an Automation & Control Systems intern, you'll contribute to automation projects that improve production reliability, reduce manual intervention, and enable remote operation of critical mining infrastructure.

## Key Responsibilities
* Assist control systems engineers in developing and testing PLC programs, SCADA interfaces, and DCS configurations for mine processing facilities
* Support functional safety assessments (SIL assessments) and safety instrumented system (SIS) reviews for critical control systems
* Analyse process historian data (OSIsoft PI) to identify automation performance gaps and tuning opportunities for control loops
* Contribute to automation project documentation including design specifications, FAT test procedures, and commissioning plans

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Foundational knowledge of industrial control systems including PLCs (Siemens, Rockwell), SCADA systems, and distributed control systems (DCS)
* Programming skills in ladder logic, structured text (IEC 61131-3), or Python for automation scripting
* Understanding of process control fundamentals (PID control, loop tuning, instrumentation) and functional safety concepts (IEC 61511)
* Enrolled in Electrical Engineering, Mechatronics, Chemical Engineering, or Control Systems Engineering (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Exposure to industrial automation at planetary scale — BHP's autonomous operations are among the world's most advanced
* Site visits to BHP's WA processing plants, rail operations, and port facilities for real-world automation context
* FIFO site trip support and Perth accommodation subsidy for interstate and international candidates
* Pathway to BHP's Automation Engineering Graduate Program and specialist control systems career track
