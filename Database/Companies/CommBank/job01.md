# Corporate Banking Internship - CommBank

## Role Description
Commonwealth Bank's Corporate & Institutional Banking (CIB) division is one of Australia's premier corporate finance operations, supporting ASX-listed clients, government entities, and multinational corporations. As a Corporate Banking intern, you'll be embedded within a sector coverage team gaining exposure to deal origination, credit analysis, and client relationship management.

## Key Responsibilities
* Assist analysts in preparing credit submissions, information memorandums, and financial models for lending transactions
* Conduct sector-specific research including industry benchmarking, peer analysis, and macroeconomic commentary
* Support relationship managers in preparing for client meetings, pitchbooks, and quarterly reviews
* Monitor portfolio companies for covenant compliance, financial covenant breaches, and material credit events

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Strong financial modelling skills in Excel including DCF, LBO, and comparable company analysis frameworks
* Understanding of corporate credit principles, financial statement analysis, and banking products
* Excellent written communication for producing client-ready documents and internal credit submissions
* Enrolled in Commerce, Finance, Accounting, Economics, or Law (penultimate year strongly preferred for return-offer eligibility)

## Perks & Benefits
* Structured eight-week program with rotations across sector teams (e.g., Infrastructure, Resources, Real Estate)
* Mentorship from Executive Directors and access to CommBank's senior leadership speaker series
* Competitive hourly rate plus superannuation contributions at the SGC rate
* Priority consideration for CommBank's Graduate Banking Analyst Program
