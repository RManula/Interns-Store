# Human-Centred Design Internship - CommBank

## Role Description
CommBank's Human-Centred Design (HCD) team sits at the heart of the bank's digital product experience, ensuring that millions of Australians can access their finances simply, safely, and confidently. As an HCD intern, you'll contribute to the design process for one of Australia's most-downloaded apps — CommBank — working on features that improve financial wellbeing for diverse customer segments.

## Key Responsibilities
* Conduct generative and evaluative user research including moderated usability tests, contextual inquiries, and surveys
* Synthesise qualitative and quantitative research data into personas, journey maps, and actionable design briefs
* Collaborate with product designers to iterate on wireframes, prototypes, and UI designs using Figma
* Present research findings and design recommendations to product squads and senior UX leadership

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Foundational UX research skills including ability to write discussion guides, recruit participants, and conduct moderated sessions
* Proficiency in Figma for wireframing and basic prototyping
* Understanding of accessibility principles and inclusive design practices
* Enrolled in Interaction Design, Human-Computer Interaction, Psychology, Digital Media, or related field (any year considered)

## Perks & Benefits
* Portfolio-worthy case studies from real CommBank product design work
* Access to CommBank's UX Research Lab with professional observation rooms and eye-tracking technology
* Weekly design critique sessions and community of practice events with CBA's 50+ strong design chapter
* Flexible hours and work-from-home days to support academic study commitments
