# Data Engineering Internship - CommBank

## Role Description
CommBank's Group Data & AI team manages one of Australia's largest enterprise data platforms, processing billions of banking transactions and customer interactions daily. As a Data Engineering intern, you'll work within the data platform team building pipelines, transforming datasets, and improving the data infrastructure that powers CBA's analytics, AI, and regulatory reporting capabilities.

## Key Responsibilities
* Design and build ETL/ELT data pipelines using Apache Spark, Airflow, and CBA's internal data platform (built on GCP BigQuery)
* Transform raw transactional and behavioural data into clean, trusted datasets for analytics and ML consumption
* Implement data quality checks, schema validation, and lineage tracking within the enterprise data catalogue
* Collaborate with data scientists and business analysts to understand data requirements and model data for self-service analytics

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Strong SQL skills with experience working on large-scale datasets (millions+ rows)
* Python proficiency for data transformation scripts and pipeline automation
* Familiarity with data pipeline orchestration tools (Airflow, Prefect) and cloud storage concepts
* Enrolled in Computer Science, Data Engineering, Information Systems, or related degree (penultimate or final year preferred)

## Perks & Benefits
* Hands-on experience with enterprise-grade data tooling at one of the largest data estates in Australia
* GCP Data Engineer certification voucher provided and supported during internship
* Fortnightly Data Engineering community of practice meetings with senior engineers
* Competitive superannuation on top of hourly stipend and paid sick leave entitlements
