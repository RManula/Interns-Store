# Financial Analytics Internship - CommBank

## Role Description
CommBank's Finance & Analytics division uses data to drive the bank's financial performance insights, regulatory reporting, and strategic planning. As a Financial Analytics intern, you'll work alongside quants and financial analysts in teams covering areas such as treasury analytics, retail banking profitability, or home lending performance.

## Key Responsibilities
* Develop and maintain financial models that track KPI performance across business units and product lines
* Extract and transform data from CBA's enterprise data warehouse using SQL and Python for reporting pipelines
* Build automated dashboards in Tableau or Power BI to surface financial trends for senior finance executives
* Assist in month-end and quarter-end reporting processes, including variance analysis and executive commentary

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Intermediate-to-advanced SQL for querying enterprise financial databases
* Python (pandas, NumPy) or R for financial data manipulation and statistical analysis
* Experience with business intelligence tools such as Tableau, Power BI, or Qlik Sense
* Enrolled in Finance, Economics, Actuarial Studies, Data Science, or Accounting (penultimate or final year preferred)

## Perks & Benefits
* Hands-on exposure to Australia's largest bank's financial data architecture and governance frameworks
* Internal analytics training program covering CBA's proprietary data platform and ML tooling
* Subsidised public transport card for Sydney CBD commute days
* Eligible for CommBank's Graduate Finance & Analytics talent program post-internship
