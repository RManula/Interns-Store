# Cybersecurity Internship - CommBank

## Role Description
CommBank's Group Security division is one of the largest enterprise cybersecurity operations in the Southern Hemisphere, protecting the financial data of over 15 million Australians. As a Cybersecurity intern, you'll join a specialist team within areas such as threat intelligence, identity & access management, or security operations to contribute to CBA's cyber resilience strategy.

## Key Responsibilities
* Monitor the Security Operations Centre (SOC) environment and assist with triaging alerts from SIEM tooling (Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel)
* Contribute to threat intelligence research by tracking adversary TTPs relevant to the Australian financial sector
* Support the Identity & Access Management (IAM) team with access review campaigns and privileged account governance
* Participate in internal tabletop exercises, phishing simulations, and security awareness initiatives

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Understanding of network security fundamentals, common attack vectors, and defensive security frameworks (MITRE ATT&CK, NIST)
* Familiarity with SIEM platforms (Splunk preferred) and log analysis techniques
* Scripting knowledge in Python or PowerShell for security automation tasks
* Pursuing a degree in Cybersecurity, Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Exposure to enterprise-grade security tooling used to protect one of Australia's Big Four banks
* Sponsored study support for CISSP Associate, CompTIA CySA+, or ASD Essential Eight certifications
* Flexible hybrid working with 3 days in CommBank's Darling Park headquarters
* Network with 200+ cybersecurity professionals across CBA's global security organisation
