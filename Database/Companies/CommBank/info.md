## 3. Workplace Culture & Employee Feedback

### 3.1. Overview & Sentiment Analysis
CommBank holds an overall rating of **3.4 out of 5 stars** based on 1,087 ratings on Seek.
* **64%** of employees would recommend this employer to a friend.
* **82%** rate their salary package as high or average compared to the market.

### 3.2. AI Summary of Recent Reviews
Employees appreciate the friendly and supportive colleagues, as well as the flexibility to work from home (WFH) and adjust schedules to suit personal needs. The modern technology and systems are user-friendly and efficient, and benefits and perks are competitive, including bonuses and incentives.

**The Challenges:** There are some potential challenges, such as inconsistent management and leadership with varying levels of support and communication. Workload and KPI expectations can be demanding, and career development opportunities may require changing roles entirely. Some employees have also noted concerns about work culture and corporate restructuring impacting job security.
