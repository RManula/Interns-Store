# Quantitative Risk Analytics Internship - CommBank

## Role Description
CommBank's Quantitative Research & Analytics (QRA) team develops the mathematical models that underpin CBA's credit risk, market risk, and capital adequacy frameworks. As a Quantitative Risk Analytics intern, you'll work alongside PhD-qualified quants on model development, validation, and performance monitoring tasks within APRA's Basel III/IV regulatory environment.

## Key Responsibilities
* Contribute to the development or validation of risk models including PD (Probability of Default), LGD, and EAD models for retail and corporate credit portfolios
* Conduct statistical backtesting and benchmarking of model predictions against observed outcomes
* Implement quantitative models in Python (scikit-learn, statsmodels, lifelines) and produce technical documentation to APRA standards
* Research academic literature and industry practice for emerging quantitative methods in credit risk and stress testing

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Strong quantitative background with proficiency in probability, statistics, regression analysis, and time series modelling
* Python or R programming for statistical model implementation and data analysis
* Understanding of credit risk concepts (Basel framework, IRB approach, stress testing) is highly advantageous
* Enrolled in Actuarial Studies, Statistics, Mathematics, Econometrics, Financial Engineering, or Physics (penultimate or final year; postgraduate students especially welcome)

## Perks & Benefits
* Exposure to regulatory-grade quantitative modelling within a Big Four Australian bank
* Access to CBA's risk model library and internal research database of industry publications
* Consideration for co-authorship on internal working papers submitted to APRA consultations
* Priority pathway to CBA's Quantitative Analyst Graduate Program ($85,000+ AUD starting salary)
