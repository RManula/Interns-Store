# Business Banking Internship - CommBank

## Role Description
CommBank's Business Banking division serves over 750,000 Australian small-to-medium enterprises (SMEs) and mid-market companies, providing lending, transaction banking, and financial advisory services. As a Business Banking intern in Melbourne, you'll rotate across relationship banking and product teams, learning how Australia's most trusted business bank supports entrepreneurs and growing companies.

## Key Responsibilities
* Shadow Business Banking Managers in client meetings, business reviews, and loan assessment processes
* Assist in preparing credit proposals, financial analysis packs, and business health assessments for SME clients
* Support the product team in analysing uptake and satisfaction metrics for business banking products (overdrafts, trade finance, equipment lending)
* Contribute to internal process improvement projects aimed at reducing onboarding friction for new business customers

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Solid foundational knowledge of accounting, financial statements, and business credit assessment principles
* Proficiency in Excel for financial modelling and data analysis tasks
* Strong interpersonal and communication skills for client-facing engagements
* Enrolled in Commerce, Finance, Accounting, Business Administration, or Economics (penultimate year preferred)

## Perks & Benefits
* Direct client exposure from week one — rare for an internship at a major bank
* Mentorship from experienced Business Banking Managers with 10+ years of industry experience
* Travel allowance for client visits across metropolitan Melbourne
* Fast-track to CommBank's Graduate Business Banking Analyst rotational program
