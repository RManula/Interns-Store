# Risk & Compliance Internship - CommBank

## Role Description
CommBank's Group Risk Management function is responsible for identifying, measuring, and managing risks across credit, market, operational, and regulatory domains. As a Risk & Compliance intern, you'll be placed in a risk team aligned to a specific business unit or risk type, learning how Australia's largest bank manages its risk appetite within APRA's prudential standards.

## Key Responsibilities
* Assist in preparing risk reports, attestations, and board-level risk committee papers
* Support operational risk reviews including control testing, risk event analysis, and RCSA (Risk and Control Self-Assessment) updates
* Research regulatory changes from APRA, ASIC, and AUSTRAC and assess their implications for CBA's compliance framework
* Contribute to model risk governance reviews, including documentation and validation support for credit risk models

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Understanding of banking regulation and prudential standards (APRA CPS 220, APS 117) is advantageous
* Analytical skills with proficiency in Excel for risk data modelling and scenario analysis
* Strong attention to detail and ability to synthesise complex regulatory requirements into clear summaries
* Enrolled in Finance, Economics, Law, Accounting, or Risk Management (penultimate or final year preferred)

## Perks & Benefits
* Structured rotation across at least two risk teams (e.g., Credit Risk and Operational Risk)
* Invitation to CommBank's Risk Academy internal training curriculum and professional development workshops
* Study support for FRM Part I (GARP) or CPA/CA stream post-internship for eligible graduates
* Inclusive and collaborative team culture with commitment to work-life balance and flexible hours
