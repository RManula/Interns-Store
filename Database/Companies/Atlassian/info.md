# Company Profile: Atlassian

## 1. Executive Summary
Atlassian is a highly successful enterprise software company built on a unique model with a historically self-service sales approach. Our first product, Jira, proved that if you make a great piece of software, price it right, and make it available to anyone to download from the internet, teams will come and build great things with it. Today, the team continues to grow across multiple global locations with products designed to help all types of teams realize their visions and get stuff done. Driven by an inspiring set of values, Atlassian's products help teams all over the planet advance humanity through the power of software.

* **Primary Location:** Sydney, NSW, Australia (Gadigal Country)

* **Specialities:** Team Collaboration Software, Jira, Confluence, Trello, DevOps Tools
