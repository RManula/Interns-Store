# Marketing Internship - Woolworths Group

## Role Description
Woolworths' Marketing team is responsible for one of Australia's most iconic and trusted brand identities, spanning advertising, loyalty, digital marketing, and sponsorship. As a Marketing intern, you'll support campaigns across Woolworths Supermarkets, Everyday Rewards, and Woolworths' Fresh Food strategy — gaining exposure to full-funnel brand and performance marketing at scale.

## Key Responsibilities
* Support the campaign management team in coordinating the production of ATL and BTL marketing materials including catalogues, digital ads, and in-store signage
* Monitor social media performance, paid digital campaigns, and influencer marketing results using Sprout Social and Meta Ads Manager
* Assist in briefing and reviewing creative agency outputs, providing feedback aligned to Woolworths' brand guidelines
* Compile weekly marketing performance reports covering reach, engagement, conversion, and cost-per-acquisition metrics

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Understanding of integrated marketing communications and campaign management processes
* Familiarity with digital marketing platforms (Google Ads, Meta Business Manager) and social media analytics
* Strong written communication skills for drafting campaign briefs, copy, and stakeholder reports
* Enrolled in Marketing, Communications, Business, Digital Media, or related degree (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Work on national marketing campaigns with budgets exceeding $50 million AUD annually
* Access to Woolworths' creative agency roster (Ogilvy, Zenith, Havas) and joint briefing sessions
* Mentorship from senior marketing leaders including Category Marketing Managers and Brand Directors
* Everyday Rewards card, generous team discounts, and flexible hybrid work arrangement
