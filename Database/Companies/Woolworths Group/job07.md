# People Analytics Internship - Woolworths Group

## Role Description
Woolworths Group employs over 200,000 team members across Australia and New Zealand, making it one of the country's largest private sector employers. Woolworths' People Analytics team uses workforce data to improve hiring, retention, team engagement, and labour scheduling across the supermarket network. As a People Analytics intern, you'll support data-driven HR initiatives that directly impact team member experience at scale.

## Key Responsibilities
* Analyse workforce data including turnover, absenteeism, engagement survey results, and scheduling efficiency using Workday and internal datasets
* Build and maintain people analytics dashboards in Power BI for HR business partners and operational leaders
* Support the design of predictive attrition models to identify high-risk team members for targeted retention interventions
* Assist in preparing presentations for the People Leadership Team on workforce trends and insights

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* SQL and Python or R skills for data extraction and people analytics modelling
* Experience with Power BI or Tableau for HR dashboard development
* Understanding of HR metrics, workforce planning concepts, and ethical considerations in people data use
* Enrolled in Human Resources, Industrial & Organisational Psychology, Data Science, Business Analytics, or related degree (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Work with one of Australia's largest workforce datasets — 200,000+ team member records
* Access to Woolworths' Workday HCM platform and internal HR analytics tooling
* Mentorship from senior HR Analytics specialists and the Chief People Officer's leadership team
* Flexible hybrid working and Woolworths team member discounts from day one
