# Data Analytics Internship - Woolworths Group

## Role Description
Woolworths' Everyday Data & Analytics team manages one of Australia's largest consumer behaviour datasets, powered by the Everyday Rewards loyalty program with over 14 million active members. As a Data Analytics intern, you'll use this rich dataset to generate insights that inform ranging decisions, promotional strategy, and personalised customer experiences across Woolworths supermarkets.

## Key Responsibilities
* Analyse Everyday Rewards transaction data to identify shopping behaviour trends, customer segmentation opportunities, and basket analysis insights
* Build and maintain automated reporting dashboards in Tableau or Power BI for category management and marketing teams
* Support the analytics team in ad hoc analysis requests including promotional uplift measurement and new store performance tracking
* Present analytical findings and recommendations in weekly team reviews and an end-of-program showcase

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Proficiency in SQL for querying large consumer transaction datasets
* Python or R skills for statistical analysis and data visualisation
* Experience with Tableau or Power BI for dashboard development
* Enrolled in Data Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Business Analytics, or related quantitative degree (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Work with one of Australia's most comprehensive consumer loyalty datasets — over 14 million active members
* Access to Woolworths' internal analytics platforms and training in enterprise data tools
* Everyday Rewards team card with monthly credit plus subsidised food and beverage at Woolworths headquarters
* Strong graduate conversion opportunities across Woolworths' data, insights, and digital teams
