# Retail Technology Internship - Woolworths Group

## Role Description
Woolworths' Retail Technology (RetailTech) division is responsible for the in-store and supply chain technology that powers Australia's largest supermarket network — including point-of-sale systems, scan & go technology, automated replenishment tools, and loss prevention solutions. As a Retail Technology intern, you'll join a technology squad building the digital backbone of Woolworths' physical retail operations.

## Key Responsibilities
* Contribute to development and testing of retail technology features within Woolworths' store operations platform
* Assist in the rollout planning and technical documentation for new in-store technology (self-checkout, smart shelving, frictionless checkout pilots)
* Analyse POS and in-store sensor data to identify operational bottlenecks and optimisation opportunities
* Support integration testing between Woolworths' ERP systems (SAP S/4HANA) and store-facing applications

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Basic programming skills in Python, Java, or JavaScript for scripting and automation tasks
* Understanding of retail technology concepts including POS systems, RFID, and IoT device management
* Familiarity with enterprise systems integration concepts (APIs, middleware, ERP connectors)
* Enrolled in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Systems, or Business Technology degree (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Unique exposure to retail technology at Australia's largest supermarket chain — see your work deployed in 1,000+ stores
* Store visits and walk-throughs with Retail Technology leadership to understand in-store operational context
* Access to Woolworths' innovation lab for hands-on testing with new retail technology prototypes
* Mentorship from senior technology leaders and inclusion in Woolworths' Tech Graduate talent pipeline
