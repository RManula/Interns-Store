# E-commerce Operations Internship - Woolworths Group

## Role Description
Woolworths' Digital & E-commerce team operates one of Australia's top online grocery platforms, fulfilling hundreds of thousands of customer orders per week through home delivery, Click & Collect, and Everyday Delivery subscription services. As an E-commerce Operations intern, you'll work within the digital operations team optimising customer experience, fulfilment efficiency, and on-site merchandising.

## Key Responsibilities
* Monitor online order fulfilment KPIs including on-time delivery rates, substitution rates, and customer satisfaction scores
* Conduct analysis of product page performance, search ranking, and digital shelf positioning to improve online sales
* Support the digital operations team with A/B testing on the Woolworths app and website using Optimizely
* Assist in coordinating promotional campaigns across the online channel, including catalogue integration and pricing validation

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Proficiency in Excel and Google Sheets for operational data analysis and reporting
* Familiarity with e-commerce platforms, digital merchandising concepts, and customer experience metrics
* Basic SQL knowledge for querying order management and customer behaviour datasets
* Enrolled in Marketing, Commerce, Business, E-commerce, Supply Chain, or related degree (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Experience working on Australia's most-visited grocery e-commerce platform (Woolworths.com.au)
* Exposure to Woolworths' technology stack including SAP, Salesforce Commerce Cloud, and proprietary WMS
* Everyday Rewards card loaded with $200 AUD credit upon starting and team lunch budget weekly
* Internship alumni have transitioned into E-commerce, Digital, and Buying roles across the Woolworths Group
