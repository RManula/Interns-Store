# Category Management Internship - Woolworths Group

## Role Description
Woolworths' Category Management team decides what products sit on the shelves of over 1,000 Australian supermarkets — managing supplier relationships, product ranging, planograms, and promotional strategy across grocery, fresh produce, and general merchandise categories. As a Category Management intern, you'll assist a category team in managing a real product range, gaining commercial acumen at the highest level in Australian retail.

## Key Responsibilities
* Analyse sales performance, market share, and consumer trend data using NielsenIQ and internal data tools to support ranging decisions
* Assist in preparing category reviews, line review presentations, and supplier negotiation packs
* Support planogram development and in-store ranging optimisation using Spaceman or JDA space management software
* Participate in supplier meetings and cross-functional range review sessions with marketing and supply chain teams

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Strong analytical skills with proficiency in Excel for commercial data analysis and modelling
* Understanding of retail buying, category management principles, and merchandising concepts
* Effective communication skills for preparing professional commercial presentations
* Enrolled in Commerce, Business, Marketing, Economics, Food Science, or related degree (penultimate or final year strongly preferred)

## Perks & Benefits
* Unique insight into how Australia's largest supermarket decides what products Australians buy
* Supplier relationship exposure — meet representatives from Australia's leading FMCG brands
* Access to NielsenIQ market research data — the same tools used by Fortune 500 FMCG companies
* Direct pathway to Woolworths Group's Commercial Graduate Buyer program ($70,000 AUD starting salary)
