# Sustainability & ESG Internship - Woolworths Group

## Role Description
Woolworths Group's Sustainability team leads the company's commitments to net-zero emissions, responsible sourcing, food waste reduction, and community investment. As a Sustainability & ESG intern, you'll contribute to programs that touch millions of Australians and help Woolworths meet its 2030 and 2050 environmental targets across its operations and supply chain.

## Key Responsibilities
* Compile and validate Scope 1, 2, and 3 greenhouse gas emissions data for Woolworths' annual sustainability report
* Research emerging ESG reporting standards (ISSB, CSRD, ASRS) and assess their applicability to Woolworths' disclosure obligations
* Support the food rescue and waste reduction team with data collection, impact reporting, and partner program coordination
* Assist in drafting ESG progress updates for the Woolworths Board, investor relations, and public sustainability communications

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Understanding of corporate sustainability frameworks (GRI, TCFD, SASB) and ESG reporting principles
* Excel and data analysis skills for emissions inventory calculations and ESG KPI tracking
* Strong written communication for drafting sustainability reports and stakeholder presentations
* Enrolled in Environmental Science, Sustainability, Business (with ESG focus), Commerce, or Public Policy (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Contribute to Woolworths' published annual sustainability report and FY2026 ESG disclosure
* Exposure to Australia's largest corporate food rescue program — SecondBite and Foodbank partnerships
* Learning budget for GRESB, GRI, or sustainability accounting certifications
* Flexible work arrangements and meaningful work with measurable environmental and social impact
