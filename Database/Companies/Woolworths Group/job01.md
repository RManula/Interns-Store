# Supply Chain Logistics Internship - Woolworths Group

## Role Description
Woolworths Group's Supply Chain & Logistics division moves over $40 billion worth of goods annually through one of Australia's most complex distribution networks. As a Supply Chain Logistics intern, you'll embed within the National Distribution Centre operations or transport planning teams, gaining hands-on exposure to how Australia's largest supermarket chain feeds the nation.

## Key Responsibilities
* Assist in analysing distribution centre throughput, pick rates, and transport route efficiency using WMS and TMS data
* Support the inventory planning team with stock replenishment modelling and out-of-stock root cause analysis
* Participate in continuous improvement (CI) projects using Lean and Six Sigma methodologies in DC operations
* Contribute to weekly supply chain KPI dashboards and present operational insights to logistics managers

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Understanding of supply chain principles, warehouse operations, and inventory management concepts
* Proficiency in Excel for data analysis, modelling, and reporting (pivot tables, VLOOKUP, Power Query)
* Analytical mindset with ability to interpret logistics data and recommend operational improvements
* Enrolled in Supply Chain Management, Logistics, Industrial Engineering, Commerce, or related degree (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Exposure to one of Australia's largest and most sophisticated retail supply chain networks
* Structured rotations including DC floor walks, vendor meetings, and transport planning sessions
* Woolworths team discount card (5% off in-store and online) active from day one
* Priority pathway to Woolworths Group's Supply Chain Graduate Program ($68,000 AUD starting package)
