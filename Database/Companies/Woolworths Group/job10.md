# Store Operations Technology Internship - Woolworths Group

## Role Description
Woolworths Group's Store Operations Technology team builds and deploys the digital tools used by store managers and team members across 1,050+ supermarket locations. As a Store Operations Technology intern in Melbourne, you'll work on mobile apps, task management platforms, and operational data systems that directly improve how store teams manage their day-to-day operations.

## Key Responsibilities
* Contribute to the development and testing of Woolworths' internal store operations mobile app (iOS/Android)
* Analyse store operations data to identify patterns in task completion rates, incident reporting, and compliance across the store network
* Support the rollout and change management process for new digital tools being deployed to stores across Victoria and NSW
* Create user guides, training materials, and change management communications for new operational technology features

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Basic mobile development knowledge (React Native, Flutter, or native iOS/Android) or willingness to learn rapidly
* Data analysis skills using Excel, Power BI, or Tableau for store operations reporting
* Strong communication skills for engaging with store managers and operations leaders as end users
* Enrolled in Computer Science, Information Technology, Business Information Systems, or related degree (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Store visit program — spend time in Woolworths stores across Melbourne to understand end-user context
* Work from Woolworths' modern Melbourne Support Office in Mulgrave with free onsite parking and canteen
* Mentorship from Store Operations Technology managers with retail technology career paths
* Team discount card, wellness programs, and access to Woolworths' EAP services from day one
