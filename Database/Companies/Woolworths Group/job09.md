# Finance & Treasury Internship - Woolworths Group

## Role Description
Woolworths Group's Finance division oversees the financial management of a $65+ billion revenue enterprise, covering treasury, financial planning & analysis (FP&A), investor relations, and statutory reporting. As a Finance & Treasury intern, you'll gain exposure to how Australia's largest food and everyday goods retailer manages its capital structure, liquidity, and financial performance.

## Key Responsibilities
* Assist the FP&A team with monthly business performance reporting, budget variance analysis, and forecasting submissions
* Support treasury operations including daily cash positioning, FX hedging analysis, and debt facility reporting
* Prepare financial models for capital allocation decisions, new store financial business cases, and strategic planning scenarios
* Contribute to Woolworths' investor relations materials including ASX announcements, investor presentations, and earnings call preparation

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Advanced Excel skills including financial modelling, sensitivity analysis, and scenario modelling
* Understanding of corporate finance principles, treasury management, and financial statement analysis
* Exposure to ERP financial systems (SAP preferred) is advantageous
* Enrolled in Finance, Accounting, Commerce, Economics, or Actuarial Studies (penultimate year strongly preferred)

## Perks & Benefits
* Exposure to ASX20 corporate finance operations and investor relations at Australia's most iconic retailer
* Mentorship from Woolworths' CFO division leadership and access to ASX roadshow preparation processes
* Study support for CA/CPA program commencement post-internship
* Competitive stipend, superannuation, and Woolworths team member benefits package---
