## 3. Workplace Culture & Employee Feedback

### 3.1. Overview & Sentiment Analysis
Woolworths Group holds an overall rating of **3.0 out of 5 stars** based on 795 ratings on Seek.
* **50%** of employees would recommend this employer to a friend.
* **75%** rate their salary package as high or average compared to the market.

### 3.2. AI Summary of Recent Reviews
Employees appreciate the friendly team environment and supportive colleagues at Woolworths Group, along with the inclusive culture and opportunities to develop valuable skills. Staff members value the shift flexibility and competitive pay, particularly for casual roles.

**The Challenges:** There are some potential challenges, including management practices that could be improved with better communication and connection to frontline staff. Employees also note that workloads can be demanding during peak periods with reduced staffing levels, and that adjusting to the fast-paced environment takes time. Career advancement may require proactive communication and relocation, whilst shift allocation for casual staff can vary.
