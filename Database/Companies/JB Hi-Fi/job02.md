# E-commerce Operations Internship - JB Hi-Fi

## Role Description
JB Hi-Fi's online store is one of Australia's highest-traffic consumer electronics e-commerce platforms, serving millions of Australians searching for the best deals on tech, gaming, appliances, and entertainment. As an E-commerce Operations intern, you'll work within the digital team managing product listings, online performance, and operational excellence for jbhifi.com.au.

## Key Responsibilities
* Maintain and optimise product content quality across the online catalogue including titles, descriptions, images, and specification attributes
* Monitor online store performance metrics including traffic, conversion rates, cart abandonment, and category revenue using Google Analytics 4
* Support promotional campaign execution including product page updates, homepage banner coordination, and price-change validation
* Assist in managing third-party marketplace integrations including eBay and Google Shopping feeds

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Understanding of e-commerce platforms, product information management (PIM), and digital shelf principles
* Familiarity with web analytics tools (Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics) and basic SEO principles
* Proficiency in Excel for bulk product data management and operational reporting
* Enrolled in Marketing, E-commerce, Business, Information Systems, or related degree (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Work on one of Australia's highest-traffic consumer electronics e-commerce platforms
* JB Hi-Fi team member discount on all products in-store and online
* Hybrid work with modern Melbourne CBD office and flexible hours
* Experience translating well to e-commerce roles across retail, FMCG, and direct-to-consumer brands
