# Finance Internship - JB Hi-Fi

## Role Description
JB Hi-Fi Group's Finance team oversees financial management for a $10+ billion revenue ASX-listed consumer electronics retailer, spanning financial reporting, FP&A, treasury, and commercial finance for both JB Hi-Fi and The Good Guys brands. As a Finance intern, you'll gain exposure to commercial finance at one of Australia's most profitable and consistent retail businesses.

## Key Responsibilities
* Assist with monthly management reporting including P&L preparation, variance analysis, and key business driver commentary
* Support the FP&A team with rolling forecast updates, category margin analysis, and new store ROI modelling
* Contribute to ASX half-year and full-year results preparation including board packs and investor relations materials
* Prepare ad hoc financial analysis for the CFO, category directors, and the JB Hi-Fi Group executive leadership team

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Advanced Excel skills for financial modelling, scenario analysis, and management reporting
* Foundational understanding of accounting standards and corporate finance principles
* SAP or ERP system familiarity is an advantage
* Enrolled in Accounting, Finance, Commerce, or Economics (penultimate year preferred; CA/CPA track eligible)

## Perks & Benefits
* Finance experience at an ASX50 retailer with consistent top-quartile return on equity in Australian retail
* Mentorship from JB Hi-Fi's Finance Director, CFO, and senior commercial finance team
* JB Hi-Fi product discount and access to The Good Guys team member benefits
* Study support for CA or CPA program enrolment post-internship
