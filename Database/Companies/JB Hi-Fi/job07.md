# Cybersecurity Internship - JB Hi-Fi

## Role Description
JB Hi-Fi's IT Security team protects the data of millions of Australian consumers and a growing e-commerce platform processing hundreds of thousands of transactions weekly. As a Cybersecurity intern, you'll contribute to JB Hi-Fi's security operations, helping safeguard payment card data (PCI-DSS), customer information, and internal business systems.

## Key Responsibilities
* Assist in monitoring security events and triaging alerts from JB Hi-Fi's SIEM platform and endpoint protection tools
* Support PCI-DSS compliance activities including quarterly scanning, evidence collection, and control testing
* Conduct vulnerability assessments and contribute to patch management processes across JB Hi-Fi's server and endpoint estate
* Research retail cybersecurity threat trends (skimming, e-commerce fraud, ransomware) and produce threat awareness briefs

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Understanding of cybersecurity fundamentals including network security, PCI-DSS standards, and OWASP vulnerabilities
* Familiarity with security tools such as vulnerability scanners (Nessus, Qualys) or endpoint protection platforms
* Scripting skills in Python or PowerShell for security automation tasks
* Enrolled in Cybersecurity, Computer Science, Information Technology, or related degree (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* PCI-DSS training and exposure to retail payment security operations — rare and highly valued skill set
* CompTIA Security+ or PCI Professional (PCIP) exam preparation support and certification voucher
* JB Hi-Fi staff discount and hybrid working model
* Pathway to JB Hi-Fi IT Security Analyst role or broader technology graduate opportunities
