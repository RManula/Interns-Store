# Supply Chain & Logistics Internship - JB Hi-Fi

## Role Description
JB Hi-Fi's Supply Chain team manages the flow of consumer electronics, appliances, gaming, and entertainment products across 200+ stores throughout Australia and New Zealand. As a Supply Chain & Logistics intern, you'll work within the national distribution and inventory planning teams, learning how one of Australia's most loved retailers keeps its shelves stocked with the hottest tech products.

## Key Responsibilities
* Assist in demand forecasting and inventory replenishment for high-velocity consumer electronics categories including smartphones, gaming, and audio
* Analyse distribution centre throughput data and transport performance metrics to identify efficiency improvement opportunities
* Support vendor-managed inventory processes including purchase order monitoring, lead time tracking, and overdue stock escalation
* Contribute to continuous improvement projects targeting reduced transit times, lower shrinkage rates, and improved fill rates

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Proficiency in Excel for inventory modelling, demand analysis, and supply chain reporting
* Understanding of supply chain fundamentals: EOQ, safety stock, reorder points, and demand variability
* Familiarity with WMS or ERP systems (SAP preferred) is advantageous
* Enrolled in Supply Chain Management, Logistics, Industrial Engineering, or Commerce (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Generous JB Hi-Fi team member product discount (up to 15% off consumer electronics and appliances)
* Exposure to supply chain operations spanning 200+ retail stores and a high-growth e-commerce fulfilment stream
* Mentorship from JB Hi-Fi's Supply Chain Manager and senior logistics leadership
* Priority pipeline to JB Hi-Fi's commercial graduate and supply chain specialist roles
