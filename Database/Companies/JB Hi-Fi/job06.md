# Buying & Merchandising Internship - JB Hi-Fi

## Role Description
JB Hi-Fi's Buying team manages commercial relationships with the world's leading technology, gaming, audio, and appliance brands — from Apple and Samsung to Sony and Microsoft. As a Buying & Merchandising intern, you'll support a category buyer in managing product ranging, pricing, and promotional strategy for one of Australia's most exciting consumer product categories.

## Key Responsibilities
* Assist category buyers in analysing sales performance, market share data, and competitive pricing intelligence for assigned product categories
* Support vendor relationship management through meeting preparation, purchase order tracking, and deal sheet analysis
* Contribute to range review presentations, promotional planning packs, and planogram update projects
* Analyse promotional uplift data and margin performance to generate commercial recommendations for category leadership

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Strong commercial analytical skills with advanced Excel proficiency (VLOOKUP, pivot tables, scenario modelling)
* Genuine interest in consumer technology, gaming, audio, or home appliances
* Effective communication skills for preparing professional presentations and engaging with supplier representatives
* Enrolled in Commerce, Business, Marketing, Economics, or related degree (penultimate year preferred)

## Perks & Benefits
* Commercial exposure to global consumer electronics and technology brands (Apple, Samsung, Sony, Microsoft, LG)
* Sit in on JB Hi-Fi's range review and vendor negotiation sessions — rare access for an intern
* Generous JB Hi-Fi team member discount on the latest tech, gaming, and audio products
* Priority pathway to JB Hi-Fi's Commercial Buying Graduate stream
