# Marketing Internship - JB Hi-Fi

## Role Description
JB Hi-Fi's Marketing team is responsible for one of Australia's most distinctive and beloved retail brand voices — known for its energetic, irreverent tone and deep connection with music, gaming, and tech culture. As a Marketing intern, you'll support multi-channel campaigns spanning digital, catalogue, social media, and in-store, working on a brand with 12 million+ monthly customer interactions.

## Key Responsibilities
* Assist in coordinating the production of weekly promotional catalogues, digital ads, social content, and eDM campaigns
* Monitor social media performance on TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube — including competitor activity tracking
* Support influencer and gaming community marketing activations and esports sponsorship programs
* Compile weekly marketing performance reports covering reach, engagement, and conversion across all paid and owned channels

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Understanding of marketing communications, campaign workflows, and brand management principles
* Familiarity with social media platforms and content creation tools (Canva, Adobe Creative Suite)
* Basic data analysis skills for interpreting marketing metrics and campaign results
* Enrolled in Marketing, Communications, Digital Media, Business, or related degree (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Work on campaigns for one of Australia's most culturally relevant and beloved retail brands
* Behind-the-scenes access to JB Hi-Fi's campaign production process including shoot days and agency sessions
* JB Hi-Fi product discount, Friday team lunches, and quarterly marketing team outings
* Pathway to JB Hi-Fi marketing coordinator and digital marketing specialist roles post-graduation---
