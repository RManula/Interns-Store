# Store Development & Property Internship - JB Hi-Fi

## Role Description
JB Hi-Fi's Store Development & Property team manages the company's national store network — from new store site identification and lease negotiation through to store fit-out, refurbishment, and closure. As a Store Development intern, you'll support the property team in researching growth opportunities, preparing feasibility models, and coordinating store opening programs.

## Key Responsibilities
* Conduct market research and demographic analysis to identify potential sites for new JB Hi-Fi and The Good Guys stores
* Prepare financial feasibility models for new store proposals including capital expenditure estimates, sales forecasts, and payback period analysis
* Support lease negotiation preparation by analysing comparable lease data, trade area catchment analysis, and landlord proposals
* Coordinate store development project timelines, working with fit-out contractors, architects, and retail operations teams

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Financial modelling skills in Excel for store-level P&L and investment return analysis
* Understanding of retail property markets, leasing concepts, and site evaluation methodologies
* Project coordination skills with ability to manage multiple stakeholder relationships simultaneously
* Enrolled in Property, Real Estate, Commerce, Finance, Urban Planning, or Architecture (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Exposure to ASX-listed retail property strategy and site acquisition at a national scale
* Store opening site visits across metropolitan and regional Australia
* JB Hi-Fi team discount, Melbourne support office facilities, and flexible work arrangements
* Career pathway into property development, retail strategy, or commercial real estate across the JB Hi-Fi Group
