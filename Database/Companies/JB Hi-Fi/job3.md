# Data Analytics Internship - JB Hi-Fi

## Role Description
JB Hi-Fi's Data & Insights team uses customer transaction data, loyalty insights, and market research to drive smarter commercial decisions across buying, marketing, and store operations. As a Data Analytics intern, you'll help turn raw retail data into insights that directly influence how JB Hi-Fi buys product, targets customers, and allocates promotional spend.

## Key Responsibilities
* Query and analyse JB Hi-Fi's sales transaction and loyalty datasets using SQL to generate category and product performance insights
* Build Power BI dashboards tracking weekly sales, margin performance, promotional uplift, and customer acquisition metrics
* Support the analytics team with ad hoc analysis for the Buying, Marketing, and Store Operations business units
* Present a final data analytics project to JB Hi-Fi's senior leadership at the intern showcase event

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Strong SQL skills for querying retail transaction and customer databases
* Python (pandas, matplotlib) or R for data manipulation and statistical analysis
* Power BI or Tableau proficiency for dashboard development and reporting
* Enrolled in Data Science, Statistics, Business Analytics, or Computer Science (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Access to JB Hi-Fi's rich sales and customer data across Australia and New Zealand
* Mentorship from JB Hi-Fi's senior data analysts and Head of Insights
* JB Hi-Fi product discounts, Friday team lunches, and a modern collaborative Melbourne office environment
* Analytical skills and retail domain knowledge that are highly transferable across Australian retail and FMCG sectors
