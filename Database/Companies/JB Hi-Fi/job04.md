# Retail Technology Internship - JB Hi-Fi

## Role Description
JB Hi-Fi's Technology team is modernising the company's retail infrastructure — from POS systems and inventory management platforms to the digital tools used by 12,000+ team members across the store network. As a Retail Technology intern, you'll contribute to IT projects that improve store efficiency, customer experience, and business performance across JB Hi-Fi's Australian and New Zealand operations.

## Key Responsibilities
* Support development and testing of JB Hi-Fi's internal retail applications including stock management, staff scheduling, and POS integrations
* Assist in the UAT and rollout coordination for new technology deployments across the store network
* Analyse IT service desk data to identify recurring technical issues and recommend systemic fixes
* Document technical runbooks, IT knowledge base articles, and change management guides for store technology rollouts

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Programming skills in Python, Java, or JavaScript for scripting and application development
* Understanding of retail technology concepts including POS systems, inventory management, and enterprise networking
* Familiarity with ITSM tools (ServiceNow or equivalent) and agile project management methodologies
* Enrolled in Computer Science, Information Technology, Software Engineering, or Business Information Systems (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Exposure to retail IT operations across Australia's leading consumer electronics retailer
* Store tour program — visit JB Hi-Fi stores to experience technology in use from a customer and team member perspective
* JB Hi-Fi staff discount and flexible hybrid working from the Melbourne support office
* Pathway into JB Hi-Fi's Technology team or IT graduate roles across the broader Harvey Norman / JB Hi-Fi group
