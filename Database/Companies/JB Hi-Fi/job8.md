# People & Culture Internship - JB Hi-Fi

## Role Description
JB Hi-Fi's People & Culture team supports 12,000+ team members across Australia and New Zealand, spanning retail stores, distribution centres, and support offices. As a People & Culture intern, you'll contribute to talent acquisition, learning & development, and team engagement initiatives at one of Australia's fastest-moving and most energetic retail workplaces.

## Key Responsibilities
* Support end-to-end recruitment processes for retail store and support office roles including job posting, screening, interview coordination, and offer management
* Assist in coordinating JB Hi-Fi's new team member onboarding and induction programs nationally
* Analyse team member engagement and turnover data and prepare summary insights for HR Business Partners
* Contribute to culture and recognition program initiatives including JB Hi-Fi's internal awards, celebrations, and learning events

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Understanding of HR management principles, recruitment practices, and employment relations fundamentals
* Proficiency in Excel and PowerPoint for HR data analysis and reporting
* Strong interpersonal skills and high emotional intelligence for handling sensitive team member matters
* Enrolled in Human Resources, Industrial Relations, Business, Psychology, or Commerce (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Exposure to HR operations across one of Australia's most dynamic retail workplaces
* JB Hi-Fi team member discount and access to exclusive tech product launches
* Mentorship from senior HR Business Partners and the Chief People Officer
* Pathway to JB Hi-Fi HR Coordinator and People & Culture Graduate roles nationally
