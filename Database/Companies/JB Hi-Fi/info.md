# Company Profile: JB Hi-Fi

## 1. Executive Summary
With over 200 stores and over 7,000 team members across Australia and New Zealand, JB Hi-Fi is one of Australasia’s largest and most trusted retailers. Specialising in consumer electronics, home entertainment, small appliances, and white goods, JB Hi-Fi strives to deliver the most extensive range, cheapest prices, and genuine service to customers every day. The heart of our business is our people who are passionate, driven, committed, and energetic!

## 3. Workplace Culture & Employee Feedback

### 3.1. Overview & Sentiment Analysis
JB Hi-Fi holds a strong overall rating of **3.7 out of 5 stars** based on 355 ratings on Seek.
* **72%** of employees would recommend this employer to a friend.
* **74%** rate their salary package and commission as high or average compared to the market.

### 3.2. AI Summary of Recent Reviews
Employees appreciate the friendly and supportive colleagues at JB Hi-Fi, with many forming lasting friendships in a collaborative team environment. The staff discounts, flexible work arrangements, and casual dress code are highly valued benefits. Additionally, the base pay and commission opportunities provide good earning potential for those in dedicated sales roles.

**The Challenges:** Management quality can vary significantly between individual stores, directly impacting communication and store atmosphere. Some employees note that cliquey behavior can make it challenging for newcomers, and shift allocation can fluctuate—with understaffing during peak retail periods and reduced hours during quieter times post-holidays.

## 4. Recent Employee Reviews

* **Rating: 5.0/5** - *Sales Associate (Moore Park, NSW) - Former Employee (Less than 1 year in role)*
    * **The Good:** Wonderfully fast-paced environment, collaborative team culture, clear operational systems, and weekly sales mentorship/training.
    * **The Challenges:** The initial induction and onboarding program could be made slightly more comprehensive for new starters.

* **Rating: 4.0/5** - *Office Admin (Melbourne, VIC) - Current Employee (15-16 years in role)*
    * **The Good:** Super chill environment where you can meet friends for life. Strong support from the immediate local management team.
    * **The Challenges:** Store happiness is highly dependent on the specific leadership team of that store. Face-to-face customer service can be physically and mentally tiring during peak periods.

* **Rating: 2.0/5** - *Sales Staff (Maroochydore, QLD) - Former Employee (Less than 1 year in role)*
    * **The Good:** General Retail Award base pay rates and a mostly good local team of co-workers.
    * **The Challenges:** Commission targets can be difficult to hit outside of the high-end HiFi department. Shifts can sometimes be reduced abruptly after major holiday periods (like Christmas) due to changing store budgets.
