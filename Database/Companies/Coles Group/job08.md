# Retail Technology Internship - Coles Group

## Role Description
Coles' Technology team is driving one of Australia's most ambitious retail technology transformations, including the implementation of Ocado's robotic fulfilment technology, self-checkout AI, and in-store digital experiences. As a Retail Technology intern, you'll join a technology squad contributing to the digital systems that serve millions of Australians every week.

## Key Responsibilities
* Contribute to development and testing of Coles' in-store and digital technology platforms including POS, self-service checkout, and team member apps
* Support the integration testing and UAT processes for new technology being deployed across the Coles store network
* Analyse technology performance data (uptime, transaction speed, error rates) and generate operational improvement recommendations
* Document technical specifications, test cases, and change management guides for new technology rollouts

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Programming skills in Java, Python, or JavaScript for application development or scripting tasks
* Understanding of retail technology concepts including POS systems, payment processing, and IoT devices
* Familiarity with software testing methodologies and bug tracking tools
* Enrolled in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Technology, or related technical degree (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Exposure to Coles' landmark Ocado robotic fulfilment centres — among the most advanced grocery automation in the world
* Store tour program giving you firsthand insight into how technology changes the shopping experience
* Access to Coles' modern technology lab for testing new retail innovation prototypes
* Coles team member benefits, flexible hybrid working, and Tech Graduate Program pathway
