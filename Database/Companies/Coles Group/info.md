# Company Profile: Coles Group

## 1. Executive Summary
Coles is one of Australia’s leading retailers, with an extensive footprint of over 1,800 retail outlets nationally. We employ more than 115,000 team members, engage with more than 8,000 suppliers, and we welcome millions of customers through our store network and digital platforms every week. We are one of Australia’s largest employers and our workforce includes in-store, corporate, manufacturing, distribution, and fulfilment operations. Our team members reflect the diverse communities in which we operate and we strive to make Coles a great place to work.

## 3. Workplace Culture & Employee Feedback

### 3.1. Overview & Sentiment Analysis
Coles Group holds an overall rating of **3.1 out of 5 stars** based on 2,664 ratings on Seek.
* **58%** of employees would recommend this employer to a friend.
* **80%** rate their salary package as high or average compared to the market.

### 3.2. AI Summary of Recent Reviews
Employees appreciate the friendly and supportive colleagues at Coles Group, noting a positive team environment across various departments. Workers value opportunities to develop communication and operational skills, along with a generally pleasant workplace atmosphere and flexible rostering options available in some roles.

**The Challenges:** There are some potential challenges, including inconsistent management practices with reports of strict micromanagement and metrics tracking. Understaffing during peak hours can lead to high workload expectations and demanding KPIs. Some employees note limited internal lateral growth between departments, fluctuating hours for casual workers, and the physically/mentally demanding nature of customer-facing roles.
