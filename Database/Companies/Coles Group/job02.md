# Buying & Merchandising Internship - Coles Group

## Role Description
Coles' Buying & Merchandising team is responsible for selecting, negotiating, and ranging the products sold across 830+ Coles supermarkets — directly shaping what Australians find on the shelf. As a Buying & Merchandising intern, you'll support a category buyer in managing a real product category from range review through to shelf execution.

## Key Responsibilities
* Assist in preparing category range review presentations, supplier comparison matrices, and commercial negotiation support packs
* Analyse category sales performance, market share trends, and promotional uplift data using IRI and internal reporting tools
* Support planogram development and store layout optimisation using JDA Space Planning software
* Participate in supplier meetings, trade show briefings, and category innovation sessions

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Strong commercial acumen and analytical skills with advanced Excel proficiency
* Understanding of retail buying and category management principles
* Effective communication and presentation skills for engaging internal stakeholders and external suppliers
* Enrolled in Commerce, Business, Marketing, Economics, or Food Science (penultimate year preferred)

## Perks & Benefits
* Hands-on commercial experience managing a real grocery category bought by millions of Australians
* Supplier relationship exposure including meetings with leading Australian and international FMCG brands
* Access to IRI and NielsenIQ market research data for category intelligence
* Direct fast-track to Coles' Commercial Graduate Buyer Program ($70,000+ AUD starting package)
