# Marketing Internship - Coles Group

## Role Description
Coles' Marketing team is responsible for driving customer traffic, building brand love, and increasing basket size across Coles Supermarkets, Coles Online, Liquorland, and the FlyBuys loyalty program. As a Marketing intern, you'll support integrated marketing campaigns across ATL, digital, and loyalty channels, working on one of Australia's most recognised and trusted consumer brands.

## Key Responsibilities
* Support campaign production coordination including briefing creative agencies, reviewing deliverables, and managing trafficking of assets across channels
* Monitor and report on marketing campaign performance across paid media, social, email, and in-store channels using relevant analytics platforms
* Assist with FlyBuys member engagement initiatives including targeted eDM campaigns, bonus points offers, and gamification mechanics
* Contribute to competitor and market intelligence reports covering promotional activity, pricing, and brand positioning in Australian grocery retail

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Understanding of marketing fundamentals, campaign management workflows, and integrated marketing communications
* Familiarity with marketing analytics platforms (Google Analytics 4, Meta Business Manager) and reporting tools
* Strong written and verbal communication skills for preparing agency briefs, campaign reports, and stakeholder presentations
* Enrolled in Marketing, Communications, Business, Commerce, or Digital Media (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Work on national campaigns for one of Australia's most iconic grocery brands reaching 18 million weekly customers
* Agency and media partner access — join shoot days, campaign debrief sessions, and media planning meetings
* FlyBuys card, Coles team discounts, and regular team events and lunches
* Pathway to Coles Marketing Graduate roles spanning brand, digital, loyalty, and category marketing
