# Finance Internship - Coles Group

## Role Description
Coles Group's Finance team manages the financial operations of a $40+ billion revenue retail enterprise, spanning FP&A, treasury, statutory reporting, and investor relations. As a Finance intern, you'll rotate through finance business partnering teams supporting Coles Supermarkets, Coles Online, or Liquorland, gaining commercial finance experience in a high-intensity ASX20 environment.

## Key Responsibilities
* Assist with monthly business performance reporting including P&L analysis, variance commentary, and rolling forecast updates
* Support the FP&A team in building financial models for strategic planning, capital expenditure approvals, and new store rollout business cases
* Contribute to ASX reporting preparation including half-year and full-year results packs and investor presentation materials
* Prepare ad hoc financial analysis for the CFO, divisional Finance Directors, and business unit leaders

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Advanced Excel proficiency including financial modelling, multi-scenario analysis, and reporting automation
* Foundational understanding of accounting standards (AASB) and corporate finance principles
* Experience with SAP or other ERP financial systems is advantageous
* Enrolled in Accounting, Finance, Commerce, or Economics (penultimate year strongly preferred for CA/CPA pathway)

## Perks & Benefits
* Exposure to ASX20 corporate finance at one of Australia's most prominent consumer businesses
* Mentorship from Finance Managers, Finance Directors, and divisional CFOs
* Study support for CA or CPA program enrolment and exam preparation
* Coles team member discount, free parking, and generous staff benefits package
