# Data Analytics Internship - Coles Group

## Role Description
Coles' Data & Analytics team harnesses the power of Australia's most-swiped loyalty card — FlyBuys (with 8+ million active households) — along with rich transaction data from across the Coles supermarket, Liquorland, and online networks. As a Data Analytics intern, you'll help unlock insights from this data to drive smarter category decisions, targeted promotions, and improved customer experiences.

## Key Responsibilities
* Analyse FlyBuys transaction and engagement data to identify customer segmentation opportunities, basket affinity patterns, and promotional response behaviours
* Develop Tableau or Power BI dashboards to track category performance, promotional uplift, and customer acquisition metrics
* Support ad hoc analytical requests from the category management, marketing, and e-commerce business units
* Participate in data storytelling workshops and present a final analytical project to the broader Data & Analytics leadership team

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Advanced SQL skills for querying large-scale retail transaction datasets
* Python (pandas, matplotlib, seaborn) or R for statistical analysis and data visualisation
* Proficiency in Tableau or Power BI for building self-service business dashboards
* Enrolled in Data Science, Statistics, Business Analytics, Mathematics, or Computer Science (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Unique access to one of Australia's richest retail loyalty datasets spanning 8+ million households
* Data analytics training program covering Coles' internal data platform and tooling
* FlyBuys card, team discounts, and free parking at Coles' Hawthorn East Support Office
* Priority consideration for Coles' Analytics & Insights Graduate Program
