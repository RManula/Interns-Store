# Human Resources Internship - Coles Group

## Role Description
Coles Group's People & Culture team supports a workforce of 120,000+ team members across Australia. As an HR intern, you'll partner with HR business partners and People Analytics teams to support talent acquisition, learning & development, and team member engagement programs across the Coles network.

## Key Responsibilities
* Support end-to-end recruitment processes for in-store, distribution centre, and support office roles including screening, interview scheduling, and offer coordination
* Assist in the coordination of Coles' Induction and Graduate Onboarding programs across multiple states
* Analyse engagement survey data and prepare summary reports for HR Business Partners and People Leaders
* Contribute to HR process improvement projects targeting reduced time-to-hire and improved candidate experience metrics

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Understanding of HR practices, employment law basics, and people management principles
* Proficiency in Excel and PowerPoint for data analysis and presentation preparation
* Strong interpersonal skills and discretion when handling confidential team member information
* Enrolled in Human Resources, Industrial Relations, Business, Psychology, or Commerce (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Exposure to HR operations across one of Australia's largest private sector employers
* Access to Coles' HRIS (SAP SuccessFactors) and applicant tracking systems from day one
* Mentorship from senior HR Business Partners and the Chief People Officer's leadership team
* Coles team discount, free parking at Hawthorn East, and access to Coles' EAP and wellbeing programs
