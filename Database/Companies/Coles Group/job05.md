# Supply Chain Internship - Coles Group

## Role Description
Coles Group's Supply Chain team operates a sophisticated national distribution network servicing 830+ supermarkets, Liquorland stores, and online fulfilment centres across Australia. As a Supply Chain intern, you'll embed within one of Coles' customer fulfilment centres or the central supply chain planning team, gaining practical exposure to demand forecasting, inventory management, and distribution operations.

## Key Responsibilities
* Support the supply chain planning team with daily and weekly demand forecasting, inventory health reviews, and replenishment cycle analysis
* Assist in analysing distribution centre performance data including throughput, pick accuracy, and labour productivity metrics
* Contribute to continuous improvement projects targeting cost reduction, waste elimination, and service level improvement across the supply network
* Prepare weekly supply chain performance reports and KPI dashboards for operations leadership

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Proficiency in Excel for supply chain data modelling including inventory turnover, safety stock calculations, and demand variance analysis
* Understanding of supply chain management concepts including EOQ, demand planning, and distribution network design
* Basic SQL skills for querying warehouse management and inventory systems data
* Enrolled in Supply Chain Management, Logistics, Industrial Engineering, Commerce, or Operations Management (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Exposure to one of Australia's largest and fastest-evolving retail supply chain networks
* Site visits to Coles' automated customer fulfilment centres featuring robotic picking technology (Witron and Ocado-powered)
* Coles team member discount card (5% off) and free onsite parking at Melbourne Support Office, Hawthorn East
* Graduate program fast-track for high-performing interns into the Coles Supply Chain Graduate stream
