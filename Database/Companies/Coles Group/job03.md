# Cybersecurity Internship - Coles Group

## Role Description
Coles' Cybersecurity team protects the digital infrastructure supporting 18 million weekly customer transactions, 830+ stores, and a growing e-commerce platform. As a Cybersecurity intern, you'll join a specialist security team working on threat detection, identity governance, or application security — gaining rare exposure to enterprise retail cybersecurity operations.

## Key Responsibilities
* Monitor security event dashboards and assist in triaging alerts generated by Coles' SIEM platform (Microsoft Sentinel)
* Support application security assessments for new digital retail features and third-party vendor integrations
* Assist in access governance reviews and identity lifecycle management processes within Coles' IAM framework
* Research and document cyber threat intelligence reports relevant to the retail and payments sector

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Understanding of cybersecurity fundamentals including common attack vectors, network security, and OWASP Top 10
* Familiarity with Microsoft security tooling (Defender, Sentinel, Entra ID) is advantageous
* Python or PowerShell scripting skills for security automation tasks
* Enrolled in Cybersecurity, Computer Science, Information Technology, or related degree (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Exposure to enterprise cybersecurity protecting one of Australia's most critical retail infrastructures
* Sponsored CompTIA Security+ or SC-900 Microsoft Security certification voucher
* Hybrid work model with 3 days in Coles' technology hub at Hawthorn East, Melbourne
* Pathway to Coles' Technology Graduate Program and cybersecurity specialist career track
