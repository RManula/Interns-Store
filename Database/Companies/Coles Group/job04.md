# Store Operations Internship - Coles Group

## Role Description
Coles' Store Operations team sets the standards, tools, and programs that guide how 830+ supermarkets operate day-to-day — from fresh food standards and rostering frameworks to team training and customer service benchmarks. As a Store Operations intern in Brisbane, you'll support state-based operational improvement initiatives and contribute to the programs that help Coles store teams serve their communities brilliantly.

## Key Responsibilities
* Analyse QLD store-level operational performance data including NPS scores, shrinkage rates, compliance audits, and labour productivity
* Support the roll-out of new operational programs including store standards updates, training modules, and in-store communication plans
* Conduct store visits across metropolitan Brisbane to gather operational insights and identify improvement opportunities
* Prepare operational performance reports and presentation decks for QLD State Operations leadership team

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Strong analytical and reporting skills using Excel and PowerPoint
* Excellent communication skills for engaging with store managers, team members, and state leadership
* Genuine passion for retail, customer experience, and frontline team development
* Enrolled in Business, Commerce, Operations Management, or related degree (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Hands-on operational leadership exposure from day one across Brisbane metropolitan stores
* Travel reimbursement for store visit days across the QLD store network
* Coles team discount, EAP access, and inclusive team culture committed to work-life balance
* Pathway to Coles' Store Operations Graduate and Retail Management Trainee programs
