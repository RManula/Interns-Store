# E-commerce Operations Internship - Coles Group

## Role Description
Coles' Online & E-commerce team manages one of Australia's fastest-growing grocery e-commerce platforms, delivering groceries to millions of Australians through home delivery and Click & Collect services. As an E-commerce Operations intern, you'll work within the digital operations squad to improve fulfilment efficiency, customer experience, and online channel profitability.

## Key Responsibilities
* Monitor and analyse online order fulfilment KPIs including on-time delivery rates, substitution rates, and Net Promoter Scores for the online channel
* Support the digital merchandising team with product content audits, search ranking analysis, and online shelf positioning optimisation
* Assist in coordinating online promotional campaigns including digital catalogue integration and pricing accuracy validation
* Contribute to e-commerce operations projects including dark store efficiency analysis and delivery zone optimisation

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Proficiency in Excel and Google Sheets for operational data analysis and reporting
* Familiarity with e-commerce analytics platforms and customer experience metrics
* Basic SQL or Python skills for querying order management and customer data
* Enrolled in Commerce, Business, Supply Chain, Marketing, Information Systems, or related degree (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Work on Australia's second-largest grocery e-commerce platform serving millions of Australians weekly
* Exposure to Coles' technology stack including Ocado platform integration and delivery management systems
* FlyBuys loyalty card loaded with points equivalent to $150 AUD upon commencement
* Coles team member discount and access to Coles' employee wellness programs and EAP services
