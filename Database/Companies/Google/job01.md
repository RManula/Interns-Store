# Software Engineering Internship - Google

## Role Description
Join Google's Core Engineering team in Sydney, where you'll work alongside some of the world's most talented engineers building products used by billions. This internship is embedded within a product squad focused on search infrastructure or assistant technologies, giving you real ownership of production-impacting code from day one.

## Key Responsibilities
* Design, build, and ship features for large-scale distributed systems serving global traffic
* Participate in code reviews, technical design discussions, and sprint planning ceremonies
* Collaborate with SREs and product managers to define technical requirements and success metrics
* Write comprehensive unit and integration tests to ensure code reliability and performance

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Proficiency in at least one of: Python, Java, Go, or C++
* Foundational understanding of data structures, algorithms, and system design principles
* Experience with version control systems (Git) and collaborative development workflows
* Currently enrolled in a Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science or a related engineering discipline (penultimate or final year preferred)

## Perks & Benefits
* Competitive hourly stipend with paid overtime for project crunch periods
* Access to Google's Sydney office campus including free gourmet meals, wellness facilities, and shuttle services
* Dedicated intern mentor plus executive speaker series and networking events
* Return offer consideration for high-performing interns at conclusion of program
