# Product Management Internship - Google

## Role Description
Google's Product team in Sydney is seeking a driven Product Management intern to join a squad working on growth and monetisation initiatives for Google Workspace or Google Ads in the APAC market. You'll learn the full product lifecycle and drive a mini-product sprint of your own from discovery through to launch readiness.

## Key Responsibilities
* Conduct market research, competitive analysis, and user interviews to define product opportunities
* Collaborate with engineering and design to write clear product requirement documents (PRDs) and user stories
* Define and track key product metrics using Google Analytics, Looker, and internal dashboards
* Present product recommendations and roadmap proposals to senior stakeholders at intern showcase events

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Demonstrated ability to think analytically and make data-informed decisions
* Familiarity with product management tools such as Jira, Confluence, or similar
* Basic SQL knowledge for querying datasets and validating product hypotheses
* Enrolled in a Bachelor's or Master's degree in Business, Computer Science, Engineering, or a related discipline (penultimate or final year preferred)

## Perks & Benefits
* Direct access to Google's internal PM community and mentorship matching program
* Attend real sprint reviews, product councils, and go-to-market planning sessions
* Free onsite meals, micro-kitchens, and quarterly intern social budget
* LinkedIn profile review and career coaching sessions with Google's talent development team
