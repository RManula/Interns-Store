# Cloud Engineering Internship - Google

## Role Description
Google Cloud's APAC Solutions Engineering team in Sydney works directly with enterprise customers to design, implement, and optimise cloud-native architectures on Google Cloud Platform (GCP). As an intern, you'll embed with customer-facing squads helping Australian businesses modernise their infrastructure and unlock the power of cloud computing.

## Key Responsibilities
* Assist in architecting GCP solutions including compute, storage, networking, and managed services (BigQuery, GKE, Cloud Run)
* Build proof-of-concept deployments and technical demos for prospective and existing enterprise customers
* Write Infrastructure-as-Code (IaC) scripts using Terraform and contribute to internal tooling libraries
* Document technical architectures, runbooks, and best-practice guides for the APAC solutions repository

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Hands-on experience with at least one major cloud platform (GCP, AWS, or Azure); GCP preferred
* Familiarity with containerisation (Docker, Kubernetes) and CI/CD pipeline concepts
* Scripting proficiency in Python or Bash for automation tasks
* Pursuing a degree in Computer Science, Cloud Computing, IT Infrastructure, or related field (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Sponsored Google Cloud Professional certification vouchers (up to 3 certifications during internship)
* Exposure to Australia's leading enterprise cloud transformation projects
* Subsidised gym membership and mental health support through Google's EAP program
* Opportunity to present intern project outcomes to APAC Cloud leadership team
