# Digital Marketing Analytics Internship - Google

## Role Description
Google's Marketing Solutions team in Sydney partners with Australian businesses to help them grow through digital advertising. As a Digital Marketing Analytics intern, you'll support the Google Ads and Performance Max teams by analysing campaign data, building client-facing reports, and developing insights that directly influence advertiser strategies across Australia.

## Key Responsibilities
* Analyse Google Ads campaign performance data to identify optimisation opportunities for key advertiser accounts
* Build automated reporting dashboards in Looker Studio and Google Sheets for internal and client-facing use
* Conduct A/B test analysis on ad creative, bidding strategies, and audience segmentation experiments
* Assist in preparing quarterly business review (QBR) presentations for Google's top-tier advertiser partners

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Proficiency in Google Analytics 4 (GA4) and Google Ads platform fundamentals
* Intermediate-to-advanced Google Sheets or Excel skills including pivot tables and data modelling
* Basic SQL for querying campaign and user behaviour datasets
* Enrolled in a degree in Marketing, Business Analytics, Commerce, Data Science, or related field (penultimate or final year preferred)

## Perks & Benefits
* Google Ads and Analytics certifications paid for and supported during internship tenure
* Exposure to Australia's largest advertising accounts and digital marketing strategies
* Free catered lunches, weekly team social events, and a $300 AUD learning budget
* Potential pathway to Google's Graduate Associate Sales Engineer or Account Strategist program
