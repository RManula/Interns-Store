# Machine Learning Research Internship - Google

## Role Description
Google DeepMind's Sydney research affiliate team is seeking a high-achieving Machine Learning Research intern to contribute to cutting-edge applied AI research. You'll work alongside research scientists on projects spanning natural language understanding, computer vision, or reinforcement learning, with the goal of producing publishable-quality research outcomes.

## Key Responsibilities
* Implement and benchmark novel machine learning algorithms and architectures using PyTorch or JAX
* Run structured experiments, analyse results statistically, and iterate on model designs based on findings
* Contribute to internal research papers, technical reports, or conference submission drafts
* Present research progress in weekly lab meetings and at the intern research symposium at end of tenure

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Deep understanding of machine learning fundamentals including neural networks, optimisation, and regularisation
* Strong coding skills in Python; experience with PyTorch, JAX, or TensorFlow
* Familiarity with research practices: literature review, experimental design, ablation studies
* Enrolled in a Master's or PhD program in Machine Learning, AI, Statistics, or Computer Science (preferred); exceptional undergraduates considered

## Perks & Benefits
* Co-authorship opportunities on internal technical reports and potential external conference papers
* Access to Google's TPU and GPU compute clusters for research experimentation
* Weekly research seminars with visiting academics and Google Brain scientists
* Relocation assistance available for interstate candidates
