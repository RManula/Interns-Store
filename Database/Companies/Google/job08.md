# Developer Relations Internship - Google

## Role Description
Google's Developer Relations (DevRel) team in Australia acts as the bridge between Google's engineering teams and the broader developer community. As a DevRel intern, you'll help grow and support Australia's developer ecosystem through technical content, community events, and hands-on developer programs.

## Key Responsibilities
* Create technical tutorials, code samples, and blog posts for Google developer products (Firebase, GCP, Android, Flutter)
* Assist in planning and running developer events including Google I/O Extended Australia, DevFests, and GDG meetups
* Engage with the Australian developer community on social platforms, forums, and GitHub to gather feedback for product teams
* Build and maintain sample applications that demonstrate Google APIs and developer tools

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Comfortable coding in at least two languages (e.g., Python, JavaScript/TypeScript, Dart/Flutter, Kotlin)
* Strong written and verbal communication skills with the ability to explain technical concepts to diverse audiences
* Passion for developer communities, open-source contribution, or technical education
* Enrolled in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Digital Communications, or related degree (any year considered)

## Perks & Benefits
* Opportunity to speak or present at Google-hosted developer community events across Australia
* Build a public portfolio of technical content published under the Google Developers brand
* Access to Google developer credits and early access programs for new product launches
* Collaborative, creative team culture with fortnightly team socials and innovation hackathons
