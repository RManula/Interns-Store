# Data Science Internship - Google

## Role Description
Google's Data Science & Analytics team in Sydney partners with product, marketing, and engineering teams to deliver data-driven insights that shape Google's APAC strategy. As a Data Science intern, you'll work on a self-contained analytical project with mentorship from senior data scientists, contributing findings that directly inform business decisions.

## Key Responsibilities
* Analyse large-scale datasets using SQL, Python (pandas, scikit-learn), and BigQuery to uncover actionable insights
* Build, validate, and document predictive models for user behaviour, market trends, or product performance
* Create compelling data visualisations and dashboards in Looker Studio or Tableau for executive reporting
* Present weekly analytical findings to your team and deliver a final project presentation to the broader Data Science chapter

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Strong proficiency in Python or R for statistical analysis and machine learning workflows
* Solid SQL skills with experience querying large relational or columnar databases
* Familiarity with data visualisation best practices and tools (Looker, Tableau, Matplotlib/Seaborn)
* Currently enrolled in a degree in Data Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, or related quantitative field (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Access to Google's internal ML tooling, datasets, and Vertex AI compute credits for your project
* Mentorship from senior data scientists and invitations to internal ML reading group sessions
* All-expenses-paid Google intern summit in Melbourne (dates TBC)
* Flexible start and end times to accommodate university exam schedules
