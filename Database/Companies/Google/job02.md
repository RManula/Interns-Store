# UI/UX Design Internship - Google

## Role Description
Google's Design Studio in Sydney is looking for a passionate UI/UX Design intern to join the team shaping the next generation of consumer and enterprise products. You'll work on real design challenges alongside full-time Googlers, contributing to products that reach users across Australia and the Asia-Pacific region.

## Key Responsibilities
* Conduct user research, usability testing sessions, and synthesise findings into actionable insights
* Create wireframes, interactive prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups using Figma and Google's Material Design system
* Present design concepts and iterations to cross-functional stakeholders including PMs and engineering leads
* Collaborate with accessibility specialists to ensure designs meet WCAG 2.2 AA standards

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Strong portfolio demonstrating end-to-end UX process from research through to delivery
* Proficiency in Figma; familiarity with Adobe Creative Suite is a plus
* Understanding of responsive design principles and mobile-first design thinking
* Currently enrolled in a degree in Interaction Design, Human-Computer Interaction, Visual Communication, or equivalent (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Portfolio-worthy project outcomes with Google branding approval for your case studies
* Access to Google's design research labs, prototyping hardware, and internal design sprints
* Mentorship from senior Google Designers and UX Researchers across the APAC region
* Subsidised public transport and flexible hybrid working arrangements
