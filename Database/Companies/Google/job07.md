# Site Reliability Engineering Internship - Google

## Role Description
Google's SRE team in Sydney is responsible for keeping Google's most critical services highly available, performant, and resilient for users across the Asia-Pacific region. As an SRE intern, you'll develop automation tooling, contribute to incident response frameworks, and learn what it takes to operate systems at planetary scale.

## Key Responsibilities
* Develop internal tooling and automation scripts to reduce toil and improve operational efficiency
* Participate in on-call shadowing rotations, post-incident reviews, and chaos engineering exercises
* Instrument services with monitoring, alerting, and SLO-based dashboards using Google's internal observability stack
* Contribute to capacity planning analyses and reliability improvement proposals for assigned service areas

## Technical Skills & Requirements
* Strong programming skills in Python, Go, or Java for systems automation
* Understanding of Linux internals, networking fundamentals (TCP/IP, DNS, HTTP), and distributed systems concepts
* Familiarity with monitoring and observability tools (Prometheus, Grafana, or equivalent)
* Pursuing a degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related technical field (penultimate or final year)

## Perks & Benefits
* Hands-on exposure to operating some of the world's most trafficked internet services
* SRE bootcamp training covering Google's production environment and reliability engineering principles
* Wellness allowance of $500 AUD for gym, mindfulness apps, or ergonomic home-office equipment
* Strong return-offer pipeline — majority of SRE interns receive full-time offers
