# Review Section Ideas for Interns Store

## 1. Student Review Section

### Purpose

The student review section shows feedback from students who used Interns Store to search, save, and apply for internships.

### Where to add it

This section can be added on:

* Home Page
* For Students Page
* Internship Listings Page

### Suggested Section Title

What Students Say About Interns Store

### Suggested Subtitle

Real feedback from students exploring internships and building their career journey.

* Home Page
* For Employers Page
* Pricing Page

## 3. Recommended Page Placement

### Home Page

Add a mixed review section with both student and employer feedback.

### For Students Page

Only show student reviews.

### For Employers Page

Only show employer/client reviews.

### Pricing Page

Show employer reviews near pricing plans to build trust.

---

## 4. Recommended Final Layout

### Section 1

What Students Say About Interns Store
Show 3 student review cards.

### Section 2

Trusted by Employers and Growing Teams
Show 3 employer review cards.

### Design Recommendation

Use a modern card layout with:

* Rounded corners
* Light shadow
* Star rating
* Profile image or initial circle
* Short review text
* Name and role

This will make Interns Store look more professional, trustworthy, and realistic for a university web development project.
