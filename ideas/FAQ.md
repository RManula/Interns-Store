# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General

### What is Interns Store?

Interns Store is an internship-focused platform that helps students discover internship opportunities and helps employers connect with talented candidates.

### Is Interns Store a real business?

No. Interns Store is a demonstration project developed for educational purposes as part of a university assessment.

---

## For Students

### Who can use Interns Store?

University students, recent graduates, and job seekers looking for internship opportunities.

### Is it free to use Interns Store?

Yes. Students can browse internship opportunities and explore available resources free of charge.

### How do I apply for an internship?

Students can view internship details and use the Apply feature to submit their applications.

### Can I save internships for later?

Yes. Interns Store allows students to save and like internships for future reference.

---

## For Employers

### Can employers advertise internship positions?

Yes. Employers can choose from different advertising packages to promote internship opportunities.

### What advertising packages are available?

Interns Store offers Basic Listing, Premium Listing, Featured Company Ads, and Monthly Promotion packages.

### How do employers contact candidates?

Employers can review applications and communicate with candidates using the contact details provided during the application process.

---

## Privacy & Safety

### Is my personal information protected?

Interns Store is designed with privacy and security principles in mind. Personal information is handled responsibly and is not shared without consent.

### What information is collected?

Basic information such as names, email addresses, and internship preferences may be collected to improve the user experience.

### How can I report suspicious activity?

Users can contact the support team through the Contact Us page if they encounter suspicious or inappropriate content.
