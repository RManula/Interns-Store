# Social Media Footer Enhancement Plan

## Current Design

The footer currently displays generic social media icons that do not clearly represent specific platforms.

### Current Icons

* Camera Icon
* Chat Icon
* Network Icon
* Play Icon

While these icons are functional, they do not clearly indicate which social media platform they represent.

---

## Recommended Social Media Platforms

Replace the generic icons with official social media platform icons.

### Platforms

* LinkedIn
* Instagram
* Facebook
* Twitter (X)

---

## Why This Change Is Important

### Better User Experience

Users instantly recognize familiar social media icons.

### Improved Professional Appearance

Official platform icons make the website look more modern and trustworthy.

### Stronger Brand Presence

Users can easily find and connect with Interns Store on social media platforms.

---

## Suggested Footer Layout

LinkedIn | Instagram | Facebook | Twitter (X)

### Example

[LinkedIn Icon]

Follow us for career opportunities and professional networking.

---

[Instagram Icon]

Explore internship stories, student achievements, and updates.

---

[Facebook Icon]

Stay informed about new internship opportunities and announcements.

---

[Twitter/X Icon]

Get quick updates, internship alerts, and industry news.

After implementing these changes, the footer will:
