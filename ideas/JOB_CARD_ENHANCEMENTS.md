# Job Card Enhancement Ideas for Interns Store

## Current Design

The current internship cards display:

* Company Logo
* Job Title
* Company Name
* Location
* Salary
* Description
* Tags
* Posted Date

While functional, the cards can be improved to look more modern and professional.

---

# 1. Add Status Badges Beside Job Titles

Display badges beside the internship title to provide quick information.

### Examples

* 🔥 JUST POSTED
* ⭐ FEATURED
* ✓ VERIFIED
* 💰 PAID
* 🎓 BEGINNER FRIENDLY
* ⏰ CLOSING SOON
* 🌐 REMOTE

### Example

Product Design Intern ⭐ FEATURED

Software Engineering Intern 🔥 JUST POSTED

Cyber Security Intern ✓ VERIFIED

---

Help students identify urgent opportunities.

### Examples

* Applications Closing Soon
* Last Week to Apply

# 6. Add Salary Highlight Badge

Display a small badge for paid opportunities.

### Examples

Create a special section at the top of the internship listings page.

# 10. Recommended Design

For the best visual appearance, each card should contain:

* Company Logo
* Industry Icon
* Job Title
* Status Badge
* Company Name
* Location
* Salary
* Internship Type
* Tags
* Application Deadline
* Posted Date
